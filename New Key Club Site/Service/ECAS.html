Here's a little something about the Every Child a Swimmer (ECAS) Program. ECAS is essentially a program set up for younger kids to help them learn about water safety. Drowning is a leading cause of death in youth, and though technology has advanced, children are still being claimed by this problem that has plagued society since the beginning of time. Surely something can be done to stop this senseless loss of life! That's where Key Club comes in. We've partnered with the Every Child a Swimmer organization to do just that--make EVERY Child a Swimmer, so that drowning goes the way of smallpox and polio as something people will eventually say "used to kill tons of kids each year"!

So what can you do? For starters, email me so that we can start setting up an Every Child a Swimmer Project for your school this year. It's really a great project--everyone gets to make a difference in the life of a child by teaching them how to swim. Instead of having to hire a swimming instructor, parents get the benefit of FREE SWIMMING INSTRUCTION and fun games for their children provided by the Key Club in partnership with a local pool. Many successful projects have been held in the past--Bridgeport, Linsly, and Jefferson have all held ECAS projects to promote awareness of drowning and to try and put an end to it. While they should be lauded for their accomplishment, it's not over yet--kids still drown, and they alone will not stop that. That's why it's up to us, ALL OF US. Just one can make a difference in the life of a child. Because of you, a life can be saved. Email me today, and let's get started on that. Let's make a difference. Let's make drowning history.

For more information, please visit the official Every Child a Swimmer website.
