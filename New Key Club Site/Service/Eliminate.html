$1.80. What can you get for that price every day? You can get a cup of coffee on the way to school, a pack of gum, or a sandwich from a restaurant when you are hungry. For 61 million mothers and their future children, $1.80 means life. Kiwanis International and UNICEF have joined forces to get rid of Maternal and Neonatal Tetanus from the planet. Maternal and neonatal tetanus (or MNT) threatens the lives of newborns and mothers every day in 34 countries.

Every nine minutes a newborn dies. That means, 60,000 infants will die from MNT in 2012. We know that life is precious-- so what is the goal for Kiwanis International and UNICEF? By 2015, these organizations will raise $110 million to ELIMINATE this deadly disease from this planet. With that money, more than 61 million mothers and their future children will have the vaccination for tetanus! Just like past Key Club International Trustee and Circle K Governor Avanti Kollaram said, "We are the Game Changers!" If we work together as a Kiwanis Family we will Eliminate this disease from this planet just like we came together to eliminate Iodine Deficiency Disorder!

8 for Elimin8. What is it? The District Board's Theme and Major Emphasis Committee has created a goal to raise $8,000 for the Eliminate Project this year. If we succeed we can save 4,444 lives! To reach this goal, average sized clubs should aim to raise $188, or roughly $5 per member, since we have almost 1600 Key Clubbers in the District! Need ideas for projects to raise money for The Eliminate Project? Don't worry, there are several project ideas listed below!

  1. Trick or Treat for UNICEF! - It's coming! The annual Trick or Treating for UNICEF is just around the corner! During the month of October, participate in this project that has existed for decades! Have fun too! You can even dress up!
  2. Walk-a-Thon - In your community hold a walk-a-thon benefiting the Eliminate Project. You can have several booths set up and even a local band, if you want! You can also have the members of your local Kiwanis and Builders Club help out! This is a great way to both promote the Eliminate Project in your community and spend time with the other branches of the Kiwanis Family.
  3. Yard Sale- A yard sale is a great way to bring all of the branches of the Kiwanis Family together to ELIMINATE the world of MNT! Just have each member bring a few donations, and have all the proceeds go to the Eliminate Project!
  4. Bake Sale - Time to roll up your sleeves and bake your best goodies! Ask a local business to see if you can hold a bake sale at their store! If you can, some stores may match the amount you fundraise!

If you want any more information on the Eliminate project or more project ideas you can email me at wvkcspecialactivities@gmail.com
