Hello Fellow Key Clubbers! Recently a program was reestablished for all clubs to participate in throughout the year. That program is called The Special Activities of the Month Program, and it encourages clubs to focus on a different service initiative each month. The clubs who participate throughout the year will be recognized at District Convention! Throughout the year the projects are as follow:

October: Reverse Trick or Treat
Some kids are not able to enjoy Trick or Treating due to hospitalization and other reasons. In October, Key Clubbers will go to local hospitals, homeless shelters, etc. and, donate candy, costumes, and their free time. It doesn't have to be on Halloween, but sometime during the month. Don't forget to dress up!

November: Kiwanis Family Serves the Community
As November is Kiwanis Family Month, clubs will volunteer with Kiwanians and other Kiwanis Family members (Builder's Club, Circle K, etc.) and volunteer at a local nursing home/ assisted living community. It will be up to the Kiwanis Family members as to what kind of project they would like to do. For instance, it might be bingo. This is a great project that may start traditions for years to come.

December: Hark the Herald Key Clubbers Sing
The holiday season is a time of rejoicing and proclaiming the joys of life. Now it is time for Key Clubbers to proclaim that joy! A Key Club will go to where the joy is needed. Whether it is a nursing home, daycare, hospital, it does not matter. It is up to the Key Club where they want to do the project.

January: New Year "Read Aloud"
January marks a new year, so Key Clubbers will go to local elementary schools and read an inspirational story. Key Clubbers will encourage the children to chase dreams and set goals.

February: Spreading Love instead of Tetanus
Key Clubs will hold fundraisers throughout the month of February to benefit the Eliminate Project! However it has to be related to Valentine's Day! Clubs can sell carnations, Crushed Ice, Valentine grams, or anything else they want! They can also have Valentine games throughout the month! Just don't forget all proceeds will go to the Eliminate Project!

March: March of Dimes Raffle
Key Club will put together a basket (or baskets) filled with goodies and sell tickets at lunch. Winning tickets will be pulled, and he/she will win the basket. Proceeds from the project will go to the Preferred Charity, March of Dimes.

If you have any questions throughout the year about these projects please let me know!

Yours in Friendship and Service,
