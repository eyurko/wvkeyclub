Governor Haden Kersting

Hello! My name is Haden Kersting and I am serving as the Governor of the West Virginia District of Key Club International for 2012-2013! This position entails presiding over the West Virginia District Board of Trustees. During the course of the year, we hold four board meetings during which the District Board makes decisions and reports on progress that will affect clubs across the state. I also have the responsibility of keeping in contact with the Kiwanis District, Key Club International, and all the presidents in West Virginia. This year we are continuing to strive for 1,700 members and we aim to build or reactivate 5 new clubs! We also aim to have 5 new clubs at Fall Rally and District Convention and 50 more people at District Convention. I believe this increased attendance will further promote the things about Key Club that so many already love - the fun and caring students and the united mission of service, character building, and leadership development.  If you or your club has any questions or if you need assistance with anything, I encourage you to reach out to me. My email address is wvkcgovernor@gmail.com.

I am a senior at Notre Dame High School in Clarksburg, WV where I live. In addition to Key Club I am part of the National Honor Society and the Robert C. Byrd High School show choir Radiant Rhythm. I also sing in the Chanticleer Children's Chorus of West Virginia. My free time consists mostly of talking with friends and singing and listening to music around my house.

I am motivated to support all the Key Clubs in West Virginia throughout the school year and I am excited to make more connections! If you are a Key Clubber in West Virginia, I hope to see you at Fall Rally in November! Again, please feel free to reach out to me. Thank you!
