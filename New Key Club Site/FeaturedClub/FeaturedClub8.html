Bridgeport High School

The Bridgeport High School Key Club!

Bridgeport High School Key Club, located in Bridgeport, West Virginia, has a passion for service! As of 2012 the club was comprised of 80+ active members, considering it one of the largest clubs in the West Virginia Key Club District to date!

The Bridgeport club has many annual service projects it partakes in, including the Clarksburg March of Dimes walk, Trick-or-Treat for UNICEF, Teacher Appreciation Day, the Spring Teacher Carwash, Back-to-School Teacher Help/Freshmen Orientation, Mock Disaster, volunteering at the local nursing home to play Bingo, the Humane Society, selling Zuls at home football games, and more! This year's elected board of officers, with Soofia Lateef as president, Sara McQueen as vice president, Stephanie Thi and Eva MacFarland as co-secretaries, Savannah Kimble as treasurer, and Austin Randolph as bulletin editor, are as excited as ever to make this year one of the best Bridgeport's club has seen!

The Bridgeport High School Key Club was also recognized this past year by the Key Club International Facebook page for raising over $300 during their school's lunchtime as a Miracle Minute for Eliminate! This is just one of many examples of how Bridgeport High's club is obsessed with serving, and really do live true by their motto: "We don't make keys; we make a difference!"
