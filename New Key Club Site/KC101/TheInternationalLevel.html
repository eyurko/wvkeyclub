Key Club is not simply a high school club, but rather a massive, international organization. Key Club International is comprised of every Key Club in the organization's 33 districts. Like individual Key Clubs and District Boards, Key Club International also has a group of elected representatives that make up the International Board.

Offices at this level include International President, International Vice-President, and 11 International Trustees. The President, with the aid of the Vice President, presides over the entire organization of Key Club International. Each Trustee serves as a liaison that conveys information and correspondence between the districts they have been assigned to and the International Board.

The International Board is responsible for International Convention and major service projects. The board also distributes information to be sent to the clubs within each district of Key Club. The President exercises supervision over the work and activities of Key Club International, presides over the international convention and all meetings of the International Board and the International Council. The President is the main representative of Key Club International. The Vice President prepares to perform the duties of the President in the event the president is unable to do so. They also chair at least one international committee, support all other committees, perform duties and attend Key Club events all over the world. Trustees interpret the meaning of the Bylaws, carry out committee directives, and maintain consistent commucation with districts to which they are assigned too.
