Want to join the world’s largest and oldest service organization for high school students? Are you ready to have fun and make new friends, but don’t know how? Join YOUR high school Key Club today! The first step is to make sure that your school has a Key Club. You can contact your school for information. You can also check alphabetically for your school or search for your club by its home division by clicking on these underlined links. If your club is not on either list, then perhaps you should consult our website for instructions on how to start a Key Club at your school.

Okay, so let's say that you've checked and your school has a Key Club. Now what? Find the faculty advisor or one of the members or officers to find out when the next meeting will be held. Gather your friends and classmates and attend the meeting! Some schools have meetings during lunch, others after school, and some are even before school.

While you will make a lot of new friends in Key Club, having friends join with you is a great way to become a new member of a club.  To learn why Key Club is a great service organization to join, check out "Why Should I Join Key Club?" Speak to current or former members of the organization about their experience with Key Club. You will be sure to hear about fun and exciting memories!

Once you decide to join Key Club, you are required to pay your dues. Congratulations! You are now an official member! You will have many opportunities to attend service projects plus the district and international conventions held each year. Remember, the KEY to service, the KEY to friendship, and the KEY to fun is in KEY CLUB!
