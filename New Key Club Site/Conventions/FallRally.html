You've probably heard by now that Key Clubbers can get a little crazy when it comes to enthusiasm for service.  Well the rumors are true, and we’ve got the photos to prove it!  For a true Key Clubber, there is nothing better to be excited about than serving others, improving the world, and being a model for today’s youth.  Now, if you’re like us, then you cannot wait for the next opportunity to show your Key Club spirit and show off your awesome cheers.  Well, have no fear! Fall Rally is here for that exact purpose!

Fall Rally is usually called the ‘pep rally for Key Club’, and what a fitting title!  Key Clubs from all over the district are invited to come and participate in the weekend fun in Charleston.  Fall Rally is an informal, two-day convention that focuses on building personal leadership and teamwork through group exercises and games.  It is also the first convention of the year that the new district board gets to lead, which makes for exciting interactions between club members and officers.

Everyone in Key Club stands to gain by attending Fall Rally.  Club Officers get ideas for icebreakers, learn how to work together better, and bond while spending the weekend together.  Club members can gain self-confidence, leadership skills, teamwork skills, and a better understanding of Key Club.  Members who are new to Key Club get their first look at the hierarchy of Key Club, gain a better understanding of the way our organization works, and really get the feel for how big and exciting Key Club really is.  The informal and fun atmosphere of the weekend also invites club members to see Key Club in a different light; the energy is a stark contrast to the typically bland settings of the average Key Club meeting.

So talk to your club members, get them pumped up, and head on down to Fall Rally this autumn!
