Every year, the West Virginia Kiwanis District holds an annual convention during which Kiwanians and their spouses, from throughout the District, meet for a weekend of business meetings, club and individual member recognition, informative workshops, and guest speakers, which often include the Key Club Governor and Circle K Governor.  There are also opportunities for fun and fellowship.  Each Division will caucus in order to elect a Lt. Governor, and during the House of Delegates, the delegates may consider amendments to the District’s by-laws or other District business,  and will elect officers for the upcoming year.  The Kiwanis District Board, the Kiwanis District Foundation, and Past District Governors also meet during the Convention.  Key Club and Circle K members are always welcome to attend, and the Key Club Governor usually presents a forum that is usually well-received by the attending Kiwanians.

This year’s convention will be August 9 – 10, 2013, in Canaan Valley, WV. Please contact Dink Yurko at Richard.Yurko@steptoe-johnson.com if you are interested in attending.
