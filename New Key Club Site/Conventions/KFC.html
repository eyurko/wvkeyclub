Every year at the YMCA Blue Ridge Assembly, nestled in the beautiful Blue Ridge Mountains near Asheville, NC, over 250 members of the Kiwanis Family from almost every district meet for fellowship, leadership, bonding and family spirit.  Sponsored by the Carolinas District, the Kiwanis Family Conference is an opportunity to meet new friends, learn important leadership skills, and participate in events that help strengthen relationships among the K Family.  Motivational speakers, team building exercises, skits, and a dance make this weekend a highlight of the year. 

The Conference is November 2 – 4, 2012.  Registrations are due on or before October 13, 2012.  Dress for the entire weekend is casual, but make sure you bring warm clothing as it could be cold, especially at night.  Room accommodations and meals are included in the registration fee, which for Kiwanis members/parents/chaperones is $228.00 for a single room or $193.00 per person for a shared room.  Fees for Key Club and Circle K members sharing rooms is $170.00 per person.

Those who have previously attended this Conference have enjoyed, not only the weekend events, but also the friendships that they make.  This weekend is a perfect opportunity to learn more about the other members of the Kiwanis Family, as well as learn about what others are doing in other districts. 

In the past, the West Virginia District has taken between 7 to 10 members to the Conference.  We have rented a van, leaving from Bridgeport early on Friday morning and returning to Bridgeport on Sunday afternoon.  If you have any interest in attending the Conference, or if you want additional information, please call Dink Yurko at (304) 669-1524, or email him at Richard.Yurko@steptoe-johnson.com.
