Parents, Buddy and Join (lovingly referred to as PB&J) is a joint project between the West Virginia Key Club and Kiwanis Districts. Started to help bolster membership in the Kiwanis District, PB&J is a project where the parents of Key Club members are invited to join Kiwanis at a special celebration where they get to see all of the cool things that Key Club does. Often, parents still think that Key Club makes keys and do not really understand the full extent of the activities that Key Club does to help make the world a better place.The purpose of the project is to make the normal family a K-Family--a family that idealizes service and works hard to better their homes, schools, and communities in everything that they do. This not only helps Kiwanis by adding more members but also helps the world--more people doing service means that every day there's another chance to change someone's life. Many clubs hold "parties" where they try to recruit parents into Kiwanis, but why stop there? Older siblings can join Circle-K or Kiwanis, younger siblings can join Builder's Club. PB&J is really just the beginning of a united effort to make YOUR family a K-Family!

If you want more information, contact the Governor or the Secretary!
